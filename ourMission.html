Our Mission.

We strive to help the homeless.

Imagine their lives.

Meet Michael, Emily, and Juan; three ordinary people living their lives on the dark streets of San Jose. Imagine being in their situation: starting your day off in the cold, wet morning on the streets, having breakfast without any food, not being able to go and having the luxuries to have entertainment. But even then, these three continue to work hard, like many other homeless, to get back on their feet. More4Homeless believes that people deserve a second chance; especially those who work for it. More4Homeless plans to host events, volunteer, and most importantly, raise awareness for homelessness. Homelessness is something that has been overlooked by society for a long time, but it is time for us to come together as a community to help the ones who need it the most.
