Our Mission.

Our only goal is to help the needy.

You don't know Paul.

But we’ve all heard of a story like his before. Unlucky financial decisions, a difficult time, and the homelessness kicks in. Even though every day is as dark as the streets that he sleeps on, Paul continues to fight, hoping to earn a better life for his wife and himself.

Meet Bella.

Raised in east San Jose, Bella knows these streets by heart. But never did she imagine she would be the one who sleeps on these dark, dangerous streets all alone, sleeping hungry every night. But even as it gets hard, Bella tries to fight back to get back to her feet.

We all can help.

At M4H, we are committed to helping these individuals. We believe that everyone deserves a second chance, and we believe we can help the homeless earn their second one. M4H plans to host events, volunteer, and most importantly, raise awareness for homelessness. Homelessness is something that has been overlooked by society for a long time, but it is time for us to come together as a community to help the ones who need it the most.
